    • Dragon Ball Series Overview

    Dragon Ball is a Japanese manga and anime franchise created by Akira Toriyama. It follows Son Goku, a Saiyan warrior, from childhood to adulthood as he trains in martial arts, searches for magical Dragon Balls, and defends Earth from powerful enemies.

    • ✔️ Main Series (Anime)

    1.DragOIP (1).webpon Ball (1986–1989)

  • Follows Goku’s childhood.
  • Tone: Adventure + martial arts.
  • Key arcs: Emperor Pilaf, Red Ribbon Army, King Piccolo.

    • 2. Dragon Ball Z (1989–1996)

  • The most famous series.
  • Covers Goku’s adult life, Saiyan origins, and large-scale battles.
  • Key arcs: Saiyan Saga, Frieza, Cell, Majin Buu.

    • 3. Dragon Ball GT (1996–1997)

  • Non-canon sequel to DBZ.
  • Focuses on space adventures and new transformations.
  • Key arcs: Black Star Dragon Balls, Baby, Super 17, Shadow Dragons.

    • 4. Dragon Ball Super (2015–2018)

  • Canon sequel to DBZ.
  • Expands the universe with new gods, universes, and transformations.
  • Key arcs: Battle of Gods, Resurrection 'F', Universe 6, Future Trunks, Tournament of Power.

    • 5. Dragon Ball Daima (2024–2025)

  • Written by Toriyama shortly before his passing.
  • Focuses on new adventures and challenges in this altered state.
